Our Story

Mutiny Labs is a product design startup from Delhi, India and the team builds and scales web and mobile products. They recently launched an in-house cloud based product called Ingage, that helps companies listen and learn from their customer feedback.

Meri Awaaz is built on the core Ingage engine at the backend and uses the Ingage Dashboard for monitoring complaints. The company enables customer centric businesses and now, is trying to promote citizen centric governance. The startup aims to build world class products from India while also working with organisations that align with their core values of audacity, clarity and excellence to help them create good products.
